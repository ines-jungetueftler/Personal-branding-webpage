About Me

I’m Inés, an Argentinian living in Berlin. During the day I work in the field of education and building community between NGOs. But since I have recollection, there is one thing I never stoped doing: draw. I love using drawing as a tool of self expression and self-discovery, to explain and understand my feelings and experiences. In the last years, I have started charing my drawing online, and that led to open opportunities along the way. I hope you enjoy my art!
